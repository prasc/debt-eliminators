Meet Our Team

Frank Kumi, MScPT, B.Kin

Registered Physiotherapist

Frank Kumi grew up in Toronto playing competitive soccer and basketball. Frank was drawn into the rehabilitation community after going through several injuries himself. After completing his Bachelor of Science Degree in Kinesiology from York University, Frank obtained a Master of Science Degree in Physical Therapy from Queen’s University. Frank has furthered his clinical education by completing courses on Post-Concussion Management, Level 1 Manual Therapy, Soft Tissue Release, Acupuncture, and Pain Neuroscience education.

Frank encourages an active approach to treatment by providing his clients with the education and exercises necessary to take control of their injury, as well as supplementing it with manual therapy as needed. As a lover of sports, you can find Frank playing basketball, soccer, volleyball, or working out when not at the clinic. Frank looks forward to building a strong therapeutic alliance with his clients and empowering them to achieve their goals! Frank has furthered his clinical education by completing courses in post-concussion management, vestibular rehabilitation, manual therapy, and acupuncture.

Jessica Baglole, MScPT, ACSM-CEP

Registered Physiotherapist

Jessica is excited to be back in Guelph and provide physiotherapy services to the community she has called home for 20 years. Jessica pursued a Bachelor of Science degree in Kinesiology from McMaster University, where she focused her studies in areas of exercise physiology and psychology. After graduating in 2016, Jessica became certified as a Clinical Exercise Physiologist through the American College of Sports Medicine. In 2019, she completed the Master of Science in Physical Therapy program at Queen’s University.

In addition to working with Kumi Physio, Jessica also works in the post-acute hospital setting in Complex Continuing Care and Inpatient Rehabilitation.

Jessica works with individuals across all stages of the rehabilitation spectrum!
